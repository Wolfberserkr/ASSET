---

## Slide 2 — What ASSET Is

ASSET is an internal, web-based training and evaluation platform built specifically for our surveillance department here at the Aruba Marriott Resort & Stellaris Casino.

In plain English: it's a drill gym for our eyes and our brains.

Every agent logs in with their employee ID, and the system hands them a 10-question mixed drill — pulled live from a pool of real scenarios across Blackjack, Roulette, Three Card Poker, Let It Ride, and Ultimate Texas Hold'em, plus shared procedure questions on chip handling and money transactions.

Some questions are multiple choice — game protection scenarios, suspicious behavior, payout rules. Others show an actual SVG layout of a table with chips placed on a bet position, and you type the correct dollar payout. It's the same call you'd make watching a live feed, just compressed into a 10-minute session.

Behind the scenes, there's a management portal where Henk and Angelo can see every agent's performance, per game, over time — and Angelo can add or edit questions as rules and scenarios evolve.

That's ASSET in one sentence: **a private, adaptive, measurable training platform that turns surveillance skill into something we can actually track.**

---

## Slide 3 — The Benefits

Let me walk you through what this actually gives us.

**1. Adaptive difficulty — the system meets each agent where they are.**
If you get three in a row right, it steps you up. If you miss two in a row, it steps you back down. Nobody gets stuck on questions that are too easy, and nobody gets buried by questions that are too hard. Every agent trains at the edge of their ability, every session.

**2. Weighted question draws — your weak spots surface more often.**
If your Roulette accuracy is strong but your Let It Ride payouts are soft, the system notices. It will quietly feed you more Let It Ride questions until that gap closes. You don't have to self-diagnose — the platform does it for you.

**3. Real visibility for management.**
Henk and Angelo can pull up a dashboard and see, at a glance: who's keeping up, who's slipping, which game categories are weakest across the team, and which individual questions are tripping everyone up. Excel export is built in — filtered by date, agent, or game — so compliance records are ready whenever they're needed.

**4. Automated recertification tracking.**
The standard is 20 completed sessions per month per agent. ASSET tracks this automatically. If someone falls short, the system flags it and notifies management in-app. No spreadsheets, no reminders, no manual counting.

**5. A Practice Mode with zero pressure.**
Separate from the scored drill, agents have a Practice tab. Pick a game, run as many questions as you want, get instant feedback with the correct answer and the explanation after every one. No score, no cooldown, no database impact. It's a private sandbox to sharpen up before the real thing.

**6. Scored drills with real stakes.**
Every completed session is scored — 10 points per correct answer, with a time multiplier up to 1.5x if you finish under 5 minutes. 150 is the ceiling. It's competitive, it's measurable, and the team benchmark is right there on your dashboard so you always know where you stand.

**7. Security and integrity built in.**
IP and device logging, audit trails on every admin action, failed-login lockouts, 30-minute inactivity timeouts, shuffled answer options so you can't memorize position. The platform is locked down in the same spirit as the floor itself.

## Slide 4 — What ASSET Eliminates

This is the part I want everyone to really hear. ASSET isn't just *adding* something — it replaces a bunch of things we've been living with for too long.

**It eliminates guesswork about who's ready and who isn't.**
No more "I think so-and-so is strong on Roulette." We have the numbers.

**It eliminates manual tracking of training hours.**
No paper logs, no shared spreadsheets, no one chasing signatures at the end of the month. Recertification is tracked the moment the session closes.

**It eliminates inconsistent training across agents.**
Up until now, every agent's prep depended on who trained them and what they happened to study. ASSET gives the whole team one shared, consistent, evolving question pool — and everyone sees the same standard.

**It eliminates the feedback delay.**
In the old model, you don't find out you got a payout wrong until a dispute happens on the floor. In Practice Mode, you know within two seconds. That compresses a year of floor learning into a few weeks of drills.

**It eliminates unverified claims in performance reviews.**
When Henk or Angelo sits down with an agent, the conversation is grounded in data: 47 completed sessions this month, 82% average on Blackjack, 61% on UTH trips payouts, 3 decay alerts in the last two weeks. No more "I feel like you're improving." We know.

**It eliminates the cost and friction of external training vendors.**
Everything is internal. We own the content, we own the platform, we own the data. No licensing, no per-seat fees, no waiting on a vendor to update a question when a rule changes — Angelo edits it in the portal, and it's live for the next session.

**It eliminates the gap between "we train" and "we can prove we train."**
Every session, every answer, every IP, every device — logged. If a regulator, a GM, or a corporate auditor ever asks us to demonstrate ongoing surveillance competency, we don't scramble. We export.
